HOW Paranormal Pulse WORKS

Most paranormal apps are random-number generators. Paranormal Pulse is built on provable cause and effect: sensor changes drive the readings, events are timestamped, and you can review the data afterward.

What each sensor does

  • Magnetometer → detects magnetic-field (EMF) changes, in milliGauss
  • Microphone → detects audio peaks (EVP review)
  • Camera → detects movement & contrast shifts
  • Correlation engine → flags when signals co-occur within seconds
